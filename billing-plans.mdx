## Plans

- **Free**: $0/month, up to 100 customers
- **Growth**: $49/month, up to 1,000 customers
- **Pro**: $99/month, up to 3,000 customers
- **Scale**: $149/month, up to 10,000 customers
- **Enterprise**: custom pricing

In **Settings > Subscription & Billing**, the plan cards show the paid upgrade paths:

- **Growth**
- **Pro**
- **Scale**
- **Enterprise**

If you are currently on **Free**, that plan appears in the subscription summary at the top instead of using one of the upgrade cards.

## How limits are applied

- Settings shows your active customer count and current plan limit.
- If usage exceeds your plan limit, MeterMonitor may truncate processing to plan cap.
- You can still access the app while over limit.

## Checkout flow

1. Click **Choose Plan**.
2. Hosted checkout opens in-app.
3. Complete payment details.
4. Return to Settings.
5. Click **Refresh Billing** to load updated status.

## Subscription status in app

- `active`
- `trialing` (paid plans only)

If a paid subscription becomes unusable (for example canceled or expired), tenant status falls back to the Free plan.

## Trial and cancellation

- Paid trials show:
  - **Trial** badge
  - trial end date
- You can cancel using:
  - **Cancel Trial** (during trial)
  - **Cancel Subscription** (from a paid plan)
- If cancellation is delayed or fails at provider side, keep using **Refresh Billing** and retry.
