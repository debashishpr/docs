## Plans

- **Free**: up to 100 customers
- **Growth 1000**: up to 1,000 customers
- **Scale 10000**: up to 10,000 customers
- **Enterprise**: custom pricing

## How limits are applied

- Settings shows your active customer count and current plan limit.
- If usage exceeds your plan limit, MeterMonitor may truncate processing to plan cap.
- You can still access the app while over limit.

## Checkout flow

1. Click **Choose Plan**.
2. Hosted checkout opens in-app.
3. Complete payment details.
4. Click **Refresh Billing** to load updated status.

## Subscription status in app

- `active`
- `trialing` (paid plans only)

If a paid subscription becomes unusable (for example canceled or expired), tenant status falls back to the Free plan.
