MeterMonitor helps you run usage-based billing operations with confidence.

## What you can do

- Track usage trends and customer-level usage rows on **Dashboard**.
- Monitor continuity and health signals on **Meter Health**.
- Review metered subscription status and usage on **Subscription Health**.
- Manage Stripe connection and plans in **Settings**.
