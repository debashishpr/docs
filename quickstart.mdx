---
title: "Quickstart"
description: "Get MeterMonitor running in minutes."
---

## Step 1: Sign in

1. Open `https://metermonitor.monetizationapps.com`.
2. Sign up or sign in.

## Step 2: Connect Stripe

1. Go to **Settings**.
2. In **Stripe Account Connection**, click **Connect Stripe** or **Change Stripe Connection**.
3. Choose one connection method:
   - **Install/connect Stripe now** (OAuth)
   - **Claim existing install** (enter `account_id`)
   - **Use Stripe restricted key** (`rk_test_...` or `rk_live_...`)
4. Complete the flow and return to MeterMonitor.

## Step 3: Verify data

1. Open **Dashboard** and confirm metrics load.
2. Open **Meter Health** and confirm your meters appear.
3. Open **Customer Insights** and search for a customer.

## Step 4: Choose a plan

1. In **Settings > Subscription & Billing**, click **Upgrade Plan**.
2. Click **Choose Plan** for a paid tier.
3. Complete checkout.
4. Click **Refresh Billing**.

<Note>
Billing updates can be delayed briefly while webhook events process.
</Note>

<Tip>
If you start a paid trial, the app shows a **Trial** badge and trial end date in **Settings > Subscription & Billing**.
</Tip>
