---
title: "Stripe connection"
description: "Connect, verify, and change your Stripe account."
---

## Connect Stripe

1. Open **Settings**.
2. Click **Connect Stripe**.
3. Authorize in Stripe.
4. Return to MeterMonitor.

## Verify connection

In **Stripe Account Connection**, check:

- `Connected` status
- `Test` or `Live` environment
- Stripe account ID

Use **Refresh Status** if the view is stale.

## Change Stripe account

Click **Change Stripe Account** and choose whether to:

- install/connect now, or
- claim an existing install.
